# Mental Health Sentiment Analyzer

## Project Overview

The Mental Health Sentiment Analyzer is a Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning project designed to analyze employee mental health survey responses and identify workplace well-being concerns.

The project uses the OSMI Mental Health in Tech Survey dataset to explore employee demographics, workplace factors, and mental health indicators. The objective is to help HR teams identify at-risk employee groups and support workplace wellness initiatives.

---

## Dataset

**Source:** OSMI Mental Health in Tech Survey

The dataset contains information related to:

* Employee demographics
* Mental health treatment history
* Family history of mental illness
* Workplace support systems
* Work interference caused by mental health conditions
* Employee comments and feedback

---

## Project Objectives

* Perform data cleaning and preprocessing
* Conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA)
* Analyze employee mental health trends
* Apply NLP techniques to textual responses
* Generate word frequency analysis and word clouds
* Build machine learning classification models
* Compare model performance
* Identify at-risk employee cohorts
* Generate HR recommendations
